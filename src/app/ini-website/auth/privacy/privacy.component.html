PRIVACY POLICY

  1. INTRODUCTION

    At Primary Offer by Chapel Hill Denham (“Primary Offer”), we take your privacy seriously. We understand you want control over your personal information, and we're dedicated to protecting your data and privacy.

    This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, and use your personal information. It also outlines your rights and choices. By reading this policy, you'll be able to make informed decisions about sharing your personal information with us.

    We strive for the highest standards of trust and transparency. This includes protecting the data of our Clients and online visitors (“’user/users”) while using our mobile applications, websites and our other online services (collectively, the “Platform”).

    Before accessing our website, receiving emails, and/or using our mobile applications, please read and accept the terms of this Policy.

  2. PERSONAL INFORMATION

    Personal information are details that identifies an individual such as names, addresses, email addresses, phone numbers and any other unique identifiable information (“Personal Information”).

    We limit the collection and use of non-public personal information to the minimum we believe is necessary to deliver superior service to you.

  3. WHAT WE COLLECT
    We obtain mostly non-public personal information directly from you or your agents whether in person, by telephone or electronically. This information may include without limitation:
    • First name, last name and title;
    • Contact information including home address, email address, business address, home telephone numbers and business telephone numbers;
    • Banking information, including your Bank Verification Number (“BVN”);
    • Non-personal identification information such as browser name, type of computer and technical information about your means of connection to our site, such as the operating system and the internet service providers utilized; and
    • Other information relevant to customer surveys and/or offers.
  4. WHY WE COLLECT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION
    All personal data that we collect and process is justified by lawful processing on the following legitimate bases:
    • You have provided consent for the processing
    • The processing is necessary for the performance of a contract to which you are a party or in order to take steps at your request prior to entering into a contract.
    • The processing is necessary to comply with our legal and regulatory obligations.
    • The processing is necessary to protect your vital interests or those of another natural person.
    • The processing is necessary for the performance of a task carried out in the public interest.
  5. HOW WE COLLECT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION
    We collect information about you and any other party whose details you provide to us when you:
    • Complete online forms.
    • Participate in surveys.
    • Interact with us through our social media platforms.
    • Download information, such as publications, or engage in any other interactive areas on our websites or portals.
    • Contact us offline via telephone, SMS, email, post, or any other communication means.
    • Access and use our websites, portals, or applications from your devices, including mobile devices.
    • Share personal data with third parties authorized to share that data, as permitted by applicable laws.
  6. DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

    We do not disclose any Personal Information about our investors, former investors or stakeholders to anyone, except when we believe it is necessary for fulfillment of our obligations to the investor, stakeholder or under an agreement with the Investor, or with the consent of the investor, or where disclosure is required by law. Except otherwise indicated herein, we will not make any disclosures of non-public personal information to other third parties who may want to sell their products or services to you.

  7. WHAT WE DO WITH THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT
    We require this information to understand your needs which will enable us to provide you with better products as well as smart service solutions. Other operational reasons:
    • Internal record keeping;
    • To improve our products and services;
    • To improve our service delivery;
    • To contact you occasionally for market research purposes;
    • To send emails about new investment opportunities or other information which we believe would be of importance to you; and
    • To improve our site through feedback you provide on products and services.
  8. COOKIE

    We utilize cookies to improve your user experience on our websites. Cookies are messages that web servers pass to your web browser when you visit internet sites and typically contain information about your visit to a specific web page, track website activities, as well as any information you have volunteered, such as name and interests. This is to ensure that you do not have to re-enter this information each time you visit the site.

    You can however choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. However, this may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.

  9. LINKS TO OTHER WEBSITES

    Our Site may contain links to other websites of interest. However, Primary Offer shall NOT be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information which you provide whilst visiting such websites; such websites are not governed by this policy. You should exercise caution and review the privacy statement(s) applicable to that websites.

  10. SECURITY OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

    Your Personal Information is stored in a manner that reasonably protects it from misuse, loss, unauthorized access and/or modification

    When your Personal Information is no longer needed for the purpose for which it was obtained, we will take reasonable steps to destroy or permanently de-identify your Personal Information. However, most of the Personal

    Information is or will be stored in client files, which will be kept by us for a minimum of seven (7) years.

  11. ACCESS TO PERSONAL INFORMATION

    You may access your Personal Information in order to update and/ or correct the details, subject to certain exceptions. If you wish to access your personal information, please contact us in writing.

    To protect your personal identification, we will require some form of identification from you before releasing the requested information.

  12. MAINTAINING THE QUALITY OF YOUR INFORMATION

If you find that the information we have is not up to date or is inaccurate, please inform us as soon as practicable so we can update our records and ensure we can continue to provide quality services to you.
